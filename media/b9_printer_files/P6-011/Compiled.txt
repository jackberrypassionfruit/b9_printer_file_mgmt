In case it hasn't been fully communicated to everyone: the reason these jobs had to be sent back is that parts were touching each other in scans that included parts from all of these jobs. When parts are touching each other, even by the most tiny amount, the CT scanner is completely unable to distinguish one part from another and the entire scan is compromised. Please, take all the time you need to be absolutely sure that no parts are touching. Double check the parts after placing the fixture in the machine; sometimes parts can shift around while you are moving them into the machine.
